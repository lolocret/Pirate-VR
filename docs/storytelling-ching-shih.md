# Storytelling - Audience with Ching Shih

## Scene Goals
- Simple and immersive VR scene
- Credible atmosphere without complex narration
- Easy to translate into interactions

## Context
The user appears on the deck of a large pirate flagship. The sea is calm, the air is heavy, and a light fog surrounds the scene. Multiple ships are visible in the distance, forming a vast fleet.

The scene should immediately suggest power, control, hierarchy, and silent tension.

## User Role (Implicit)
The user is not a hero. Their role is left ambiguous: visitor, emissary, prisoner, or new arrival. The scene should communicate the feeling of being observed, judged, and awaited.

## Central Character: Ching Shih
- Position: standing in front of the user, slightly elevated or very stable
- Distance: comfortable VR distance
- Posture: calm, dominant, minimal movement
- Expression: authority and control, never aggressive

## Atmosphere
- Sea: slow and regular movement
- Fog: light, soft depth
- Light: soft or dramatic
- Sound: wind, waves, wood and rigging creaks

## Living Elements
Secondary pirates on the deck perform subtle looped actions:
- standing guard
- handling ropes
- watching the sea
- consulting a map

They should never draw more attention than Ching Shih.

## Surrounding Fleet
Distant ships are partially swallowed by fog, slightly animated, silent and imposing. The effect should reinforce the feeling of being at the center of a massive power.

## Interactions (A-Frame Friendly)
- Gaze at Ching Shih: slight head turn or micro motion
- Approach: ambient focus increases, tension rises
- Deck objects: map, sabre, flag, crate with simple feedback
- Looking toward fleet: stronger atmosphere and scale

## Global Emotional Intent
Curiosity, mild intimidation, fascination, and importance of the moment, without explicit dialogue.
